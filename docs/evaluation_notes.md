# BrandMind Evaluation Notes

## Baseline Benchmark (20 cases)
- BrandMind WCAG: 0.95 (best among all systems)
- BrandMind Coherence: 4.32/5
- Zero-shot WCAG: 0.60
- RAG-only WCAG: 0.75
- Fontjoy WCAG: 0.00

## Human Study (20 participants, 5 brands)
- Kit A (BrandMind) preferred: 49%
- Kit B (Zero-shot) preferred: 21%
- About the same: 30%
- Avg Kit A rating: 3.70/5
- Avg Kit B rating: 2.90/5

## Key Finding
BrandMind achieves highest WCAG compliance and is preferred
by human evaluators despite lower automated CSR score.
