# Human Study Design

## Overview
Blind A/B preference study comparing BrandMind vs Zero-shot baseline.

## Setup
- 5 brands randomly selected from 20-case benchmark
- Kit A = BrandMind output
- Kit B = Zero-shot baseline output
- System labels hidden from participants
- Assignment order randomized

## Participants
- Total: 20
- No experience: 2
- Slightly familiar: 4
- Moderately familiar: 6
- Professional experience: 8

## Survey Link
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfWlb4Ib3UMLXHb-9DxraWrMaFdzDVFbkIW29NN_rB_96jtDg/viewform

## Questions Per Brand
1. Which kit feels more appropriate? (Kit A / Kit B / About the same)
2. Rate Kit A overall fit (1-5)
3. Rate Kit B overall fit (1-5)
